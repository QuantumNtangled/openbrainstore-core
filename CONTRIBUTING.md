# Contributing

Thanks for your interest in OpenBrainStore.

This repository is published for **transparency and self-hosting**. At this
stage the project is developed in-house and we are **not accepting external
code contributions** — issues and pull requests may not be reviewed or merged.
That will likely change as the project matures; watch the repo for updates.

You are very welcome to:

- **Self-host it** under the [PolyForm Small Business License](LICENSE.md) if
  you qualify (individuals, and companies under 100 people / $1M revenue).
- **Report security issues** privately — see [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md).
- **Ask licensing or partnership questions** via the contact form at
  [openbrainstore.com/#contact](https://openbrainstore.com/#contact).

If you fork it, please keep the `Required Notice` copyright line intact, as the
license requires.
